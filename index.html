About

Daniel Kincaid

Daniel Kincaid is a singer-songwriter whose music lives in the space between confession and craft — pop songwriting with enough weight to mean something, enough melody to stay with you.

Influenced by the late 80s and early 90s tradition of artists who wrote big emotional songs and weren't apologetic about it, Daniel brings that same ambition to his own material. Every track is built around a real moment, a real feeling, told as plainly and as beautifully as he knows how.

Released through Hello Texas Records, his growing catalog of singles and his debut album Running Out of Reasons represent an artist who's still early but already knows exactly what he's here to say.
